## What Went Well

- **Preamble concept validated:** ShuHaRi as developer experience level (not agent skill) is clean and clear. Universal definitions extracted, skills only declare behavior deltas.
- **Compression was easier than expected:** ADR-040 targets (≤150 lines) were comfortably achieved. Most skills had 30-60% removable bloat (philosophy, inline templates, Notes sections).
- **Session pair as pilot was a good choice:** Refactoring session-start and session-close together validated that the preamble extraction works for complementary skills.
- **Pattern compounding (PAT-E-442):** Each successive pilot was faster. session-start established the pattern, session-close confirmed it, story-implement was the most aggressive compression but the most mechanical.

## What Could Improve

- **No automated substance ratio measurement:** We counted lines and sections but didn't quantify the ≥80% substance ratio objectively. For S250.4, consider a simple heuristic: lines that contain code, tables, or actionable instructions vs lines that are blank, commentary, or philosophy.
- **Preamble loading mechanism undefined:** The preamble exists as a file but there's no mechanism to ensure it's loaded in every session. Currently it's a reference doc. S250.4 should address how `rai skill validate` or CLAUDE.md ensures it's loaded.

## Heutagogical Checkpoint

### What did you learn?
- The biggest bloat sources in skills are inline templates and Notes/philosophy sections, not the steps themselves. Steps are mostly substance.
- Fractional step numbering (0, 0.1, 1, 1.5) is a smell for organic growth — merging into clean integers is always possible and always clearer.

### What would you change about the process?
- Nothing significant. The story flowed well: infrastructure first (preamble + template), then pilots in order of increasing compression difficulty.

### Are there improvements for the framework?
- The contract template itself is a framework improvement. S250.2/S250.3 should be mechanical application of this pattern.

### What are you more capable of now?
- Clear criteria for what's substance vs bloat in a skill file. The 7-section structure is now internalized.

## Patterns

- **Content refactoring compounds faster than code refactoring:** 3 skill refactors in sequence, each faster than the previous. No test overhead (markdown, not code). PAT-E-442 applies even more strongly here.
- **ShuHaRi clarification:** ShuHaRi is the developer's experience level with RaiSE, not the agent's capability. This distinction matters for the preamble and all future skills.
