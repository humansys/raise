## What to Improve

- **TDD should include cross-reference validation from the start.** When field A references field B by name (e.g., `default_instance: nonexistent`), test that B exists during the RED phase, not in review.
- **health() contract should be explicit.** The "never raise" contract is implicit — consider adding it as a guardrail or documenting it in the protocol.
