### F12: Skill compression under orchestration (critical)

**Location:** Both orchestrators, Step 2 (Execute Skill Chain)
**Issue:** The instruction "Each skill invocation follows its own SKILL.md completely" is too weak. In practice, when running through the chain, skills get compressed — steps are skipped, artifacts are summarized instead of produced, and the orchestrator treats skills as checkboxes rather than full executions.
**Evidence:** In this very story (S346.1), phases 2-3 (design/plan) were compressed into quick writes, phases 5-6 (AR/QR) were skipped entirely with "not applicable" rationale, and phase 7 (story-review) skipped pattern reinforcement.
**Impact:** Critical — defeats the purpose of the skill chain. Skills lose their value when compressed.
**Fix applied:** Added explicit "Full execution rule" to both orchestrators requiring: (1) load the skill's SKILL.md from file, (2) execute every step sequentially, (3) produce all specified artifacts. Added quality checklist items enforcing no compression.
