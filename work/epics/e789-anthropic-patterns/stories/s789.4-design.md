## Design Decisions

### D1: Two deep reads sufficient
Art.6 and Art.7 are the most empirically grounded multi-agent articles. Art.6 has metrics (90.2% performance). Art.7 has concrete coordination primitives (file-lock, bare-git). Art.1 provides the pattern taxonomy but was already read in S789.1/S789.2.

Same approach as S789.3/G11 (PAT-E-013): use the gap as concrete validation for the proposed principle change. "Agent dispatches work to other agents" — where does this fall in Human/Machine/Shared classification?
