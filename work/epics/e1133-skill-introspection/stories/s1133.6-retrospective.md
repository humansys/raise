## Patterns

### PAT: First-deployment adoption requires enforcement, not suggestion
**Context:** introspection, adoption, AI agents
**Pattern:** When deploying a new protocol to AI agents via skill markers, suggestive language ("follow protocol...") gets interpreted as optional. Agents optimize for task completion and skip ceremony they judge unnecessary. Mandatory language + quality checklist gates + CLI tooling are needed for reliable adoption.
**Learned from:** S1133.6 — E1134 produced 0/4 records despite having markers in all skills.
