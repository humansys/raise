# S1130.1 Retrospective

## Summary

Implemented `ConfluenceDiscovery` service and `ConfluenceSpaceMap` model. 9 tests, all passing. QR found a critical semantic bug (space_key used where page_id expected) — fixed before merge.

## What went well

- TDD caught the model and error-handling shape correctly
- QR caught a semantic bug invisible to mocks — `get_page_children(space.key)` always returns empty in production
- Fix was clean: added `get_space_homepage_id()` to `ConfluenceClient`, resolved homepage before querying children

## What to improve

- **Mock contract enforcement**: mocks should validate parameter types/shapes, not just accept anything. The permissive mock hid the page_id vs space_key mismatch.
- **Scope drift**: added `get_space_homepage_id()` to `ConfluenceClient` which was originally "out of scope". The scope was wrong — discovery genuinely needed this method.

## Patterns

- **PAT: QR catches semantic bugs mocks hide** — permissive mocks pass regardless of parameter correctness. QR with API contract knowledge catches what TDD misses.
- **PAT: Confluence space → homepage → children** — to get top-level pages of a space, resolve homepage ID via `get_home_page_of_space()`, then `get_page_children(homepage_id)`.

- Estimated: 25 min (2 tasks)
- Actual: ~35 min (QR fix added ~10 min)
- Ratio: 1.4x — QR overhead expected for first story
