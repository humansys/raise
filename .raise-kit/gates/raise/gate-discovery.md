# Checklist: Gate-Discovery (PRD Validation)

**Feature**: [Discovery Phase]
**Source**: `.raise/src/gates/gate-discovery.md`

## Content Quality (Must Pass)

- [ ] GD001 [Requirement] Problem Statement describes who has the problem, its impact, and why now.
- [ ] GD002 [Requirement] Goals have at least one success metric with a numerical target.
- [ ] GD003 [Requirement] Scope In-Scope and Out-of-Scope lists are non-empty and explicit.
- [ ] GD004 [Requirement] Functional requirements are testable (can be expressed as Given/When/Then).
- [ ] GD005 [Requirement] Non-functional requirements (NFRs) are quantified (time, %, concurrency).
- [ ] GD006 [Requirement] At least 3 risks are documented with associated mitigation strategies.
- [ ] GD007 [Process] Evidence of stakeholder approval is present or documented.

## Recommendation Quality (Should Pass)

- [ ] GD008 [Requirement] At least 3 explicit assumptions are documented.
- [ ] GD009 [Requirement] Requirements use MoSCoW prioritization (Must, Should, Could, Won't).
- [ ] GD010 [Requirement] No "TBD", placeholders, or "pending" markers remain in the document.

## Final Approval
- [ ] GD-DONE [Status] All mandatory criteria met. Ready for Solution Vision.
